OpenDLP-LK Data Champion Certification

The OpenDLP-LK Data Champion Certification validates your expertise in Data Loss Prevention and PDPA compliance. This recognized credential demonstrates your ability to lead DLP initiatives, train end users, and maintain compliance within your organization.

Certification Value

Professional Recognition
Industry-recognized credential for DLP expertise
Career Advancement
Differentiate yourself in security roles
Compliance Assurance
Demonstrate PDPA knowledge to stakeholders

Certification Levels

Choose the certification level that matches your role and experience.

Associate Data Champion

Entry-level certification for new champions

Level 1

Prerequisites:

  • • Completion of Foundation training path
  • • 6+ months employment in current organization
  • • Manager recommendation

Exam Format:

  • • 60 multiple-choice questions
  • • 2 practical scenario exercises
  • • 90 minutes duration
  • • 75% passing score

Professional Data Champion

Advanced certification for experienced champions

Level 2

Prerequisites:

  • • Associate certification OR 1+ year as champion
  • • Completion of Advanced or Leadership path
  • • Documented champion activities (portfolio)

Exam Format:

  • • 80 multiple-choice questions
  • • 4 complex scenario exercises
  • • 120 minutes duration
  • • 80% passing score

Master Data Champion

Elite certification for program leaders

Level 3

Prerequisites:

  • • Professional certification
  • • 2+ years as Senior/Lead Champion
  • • Case study submission
  • • Peer review endorsement

Exam Format:

  • • Comprehensive case study analysis
  • • Oral examination (45 minutes)
  • • Program implementation plan
  • • Panel review

Certification Process

1

Complete Required Training

Finish your chosen learning path (Foundation, Advanced, or Leadership) with all modules and labs.

Timeline: 8-12 weeks depending on path
2

Submit Application

Complete the certification application with required documentation (manager approval, portfolio, etc.).

Processing: 1-2 weeks for application review
3

Schedule Examination

Choose your exam date and format (online proctored or in-person testing center).

Availability: Monthly exam windows
4

Pass Examination

Successfully complete the written exam and practical scenarios with the required passing score.

Results: Immediate preliminary score; official results within 5 business days
5

Receive Certification

Get your official certificate, digital badge, and access to the champion alumni network.

Deliverables: Certificate (PDF + physical), LinkedIn badge, champion toolkit

Exam Content Areas (Associate Level)

The certification exam covers the following domains:

Domain 1: DLP Fundamentals

25%
  • • DLP concepts, terminology, and architecture
  • • Data classification and sensitivity levels
  • • Common data types and patterns (NIC, banking, etc.)

Domain 2: PDPA Compliance

20%
  • • PDPA requirements and principles
  • • Data subject rights and organization obligations
  • • Breach notification procedures

Domain 3: Policy Management

25%
  • • Creating and maintaining DLP policies
  • • Detection rules and content inspection
  • • Policy actions and response mechanisms

Domain 4: Incident Response

20%
  • • Incident classification and severity assessment
  • • Investigation and evidence collection
  • • Remediation and escalation procedures

Domain 5: Training & Communication

10%
  • • Adult learning principles and training techniques
  • • Creating effective training materials
  • • Stakeholder communication strategies

Certification Maintenance

Keep your certification current and demonstrate ongoing professional development.

Renewal Requirements

Certifications are valid for 2 years. To renew:

  • Earn 20 Continuing Education Units (CEUs)
  • Submit annual champion activity report
  • Pay renewal fee (LKR 5,000 / USD 25)
  • Maintain active champion status

Earning CEUs

Approved activities for continuing education:

  • Advanced training courses (1 CEU per hour)
  • Security conferences and webinars (1 CEU per hour)
  • Published articles or case studies (5 CEUs each)
  • Mentoring new champions (10 CEUs per year)
