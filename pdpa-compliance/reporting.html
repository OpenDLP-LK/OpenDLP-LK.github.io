PDPA Compliance Reporting

Templates and guidance for compliance documentation

Required Reports

Annual Compliance Report

Comprehensive annual report to Data Protection Officer and executive leadership on PDPA compliance status.

Required Content:

  • • Data processing activities summary
  • • DLP effectiveness metrics
  • • Incidents and breaches (if any)
  • • Training completion rates
  • • Audit findings and remediation

Quarterly Security Reports

Regular reports on DLP system performance and data protection effectiveness.

Key Metrics:

  • • Policy violations detected and prevented
  • • False positive rate
  • • User training and awareness metrics
  • • System uptime and performance

Data Breach Notification

Required within 72 hours of breach discovery to PDPA Authority.

Must Include:

  • • Nature of the breach
  • • Categories and approximate number of affected individuals
  • • Likely consequences
  • • Measures taken or proposed to address the breach
  • • DPO contact information
