Popular Data Patterns

Most commonly used patterns for Sri Lankan data detection

View All Patterns

National Identity Card (NIC)

Old and new NIC formats

Combined Pattern:

^(\d{9}[VvXx]|\d{12})$

Detects both old format (9 digits + V/X) and new format (12 digits)

Mobile Numbers

Sri Lankan mobile phone numbers

Standard Format:

^0[7][0-9]{8}$

Matches Sri Lankan mobile numbers starting with 07X

Passport Numbers

Sri Lankan passport format

Pattern:

^[A-Z]\d{7}$

One letter followed by 7 digits (e.g., N1234567)

Bank Account Numbers

Standard account format

Pattern:

^\d{10,16}$

10-16 digits (varies by bank)
