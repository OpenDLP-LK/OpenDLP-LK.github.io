Community Events

Join our workshops, webinars, and community meetups to learn, network, and advance your DLP expertise alongside other data protection professionals in Sri Lanka.

All events are free and open to the community. Stay connected to learn about upcoming events!

Types of Events

Workshops

Hands-on sessions covering DLP implementation, configuration, and best practices with practical exercises.

Duration: 2-4 hours
Format: Interactive, hands-on

Webinars

Online presentations by experts covering specific topics, trends, and advanced DLP concepts with Q&A sessions.

Duration: 1-2 hours
Format: Presentation + Q&A

Meetups

In-person or virtual gatherings for networking, knowledge sharing, and community building among DLP practitioners.

Duration: 2-3 hours
Format: Networking + talks

Hackathons

Collaborative coding events focused on developing DLP tools, patterns, and solutions for Sri Lankan organizations.

Duration: 1-2 days
Format: Team collaboration

Training Sessions

Structured learning programs covering comprehensive DLP topics, from beginner to advanced levels.

Duration: Multi-session
Format: Course-based learning

Panel Discussions

Expert panels discussing industry trends, challenges, and best practices in data protection and DLP.

Duration: 1-2 hours
Format: Panel + audience Q&A

Propose an Event

Have an idea for a workshop, webinar, or community event? We'd love to hear from you! Community-led events are a great way to share expertise and strengthen our network.

What You Can Propose

  • Technical workshops or tutorials
  • Industry experience sharing sessions
  • PDPA compliance discussions
  • Tool demonstrations
  • Panel discussions or Q&A sessions

Event Proposal Guidelines

  • Relevant to DLP or data protection
  • Accessible to community members
  • Clear learning objectives
  • Realistic time commitment
  • Follows code of conduct
