Training Overview

Our comprehensive 12-week Data Champion training program equips participants with the knowledge, skills, and tools to effectively lead DLP adoption in their departments. The curriculum combines theoretical foundations, hands-on practice, and real-world application.

Detailed Curriculum (Foundation Path)

Module 1: DLP & PDPA Foundations (Weeks 1-2) 8 hours

  • 1.1 Introduction to Data Loss Prevention - Concepts, threats, and controls
  • 1.2 PDPA Overview - Sri Lankan data protection legal framework
  • 1.3 Data Classification - Sensitivity levels and handling requirements
  • 1.4 Common Data Types - NIC, banking, health, employee data patterns
  • Lab: Classify sample datasets using OpenDLP-LK patterns

Module 2: DLP Policy Management (Weeks 3-4) 10 hours

  • 2.1 Policy Framework - Creating effective DLP policies
  • 2.2 Detection Rules - Pattern matching and content inspection
  • 2.3 Actions & Responses - Block, monitor, alert, encrypt
  • 2.4 Department-Specific Policies - HR, Finance, Operations examples
  • Lab: Create and test a department-specific DLP policy

Module 3: Incident Response (Weeks 5-6) 8 hours

  • 3.1 Incident Types - Accidental vs. malicious, severity classification
  • 3.2 Investigation Process - Evidence collection and analysis
  • 3.3 Response Procedures - Escalation paths and remediation
  • 3.4 Reporting Requirements - Internal and PDPA breach notification
  • Lab: Investigate and respond to simulated DLP incidents

Module 4: Training & Communication (Weeks 7-9) 10 hours

  • 4.1 Adult Learning Principles - Effective training techniques
  • 4.2 Creating Training Materials - Presentations, quick guides, videos
  • 4.3 Stakeholder Communication - Tailoring messages for different audiences
  • 4.4 Change Management - Overcoming resistance and building buy-in
  • Lab: Develop and deliver a 15-minute training session

Module 5: Operations & Certification (Weeks 10-12) 12 hours

  • 5.1 Daily Operations - Monitoring, reporting, continuous improvement
  • 5.2 Metrics & KPIs - Measuring champion program success
  • 5.3 Capstone Project - Implement DLP in your department
  • 5.4 Final Assessment - Written exam + practical demonstration
  • Deliverable: Department DLP implementation plan
